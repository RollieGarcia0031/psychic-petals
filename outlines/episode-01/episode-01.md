# Episode 1: The Girl Who Inspired the Songs

## Overview
Damien, a boy who can manifest his imagination into physical objects, navigates a high school life defined by emotional distance and the belief that people only want him for his power. His worldview is challenged by Dahlia, whose genuine affection he doesn't know how to accept, leading to a journey of secret songwriting, failed courage, and eventual self-reflection.

## Themes
* **Isolation vs. Connection:** The safety of distance versus the risk of being known.
* **Transactional Love:** The belief that attention always comes with strings attached.
* **The Power of the Unspoken:** Feelings that can only be expressed through music and metaphors.
* **Loss as a Catalyst:** How silence and fear can lead to irreversible regret.

## Major Milestones
1. **The Introduction (Ch 1-2):** Establishing Damien's "blind spots" and his rule of conditions.
2. **The Contradiction (Ch 3-4):** Dahlia's entry and Damien's silent testing of her sincerity.
3. **The Harmonic Bond (Ch 5-6):** Connection through the school band and the start of the secret songs.
4. **The Boundary (Ch 7):** Dahlia's plea for clarity and Damien's failure to act.
5. **The Pull Away (Ch 8-9):** The arrival of a more open rival and Damien's descent into anonymous confession.
6. **The Final Resonance (Ch 10):** Damien's disappearance, the leaked recording, and the bittersweet peace.

## Character Goals
* **Damien:** To protect himself from being "used" while secretly longing for a connection that doesn't require his power.
* **Dahlia:** To understand the boy behind the silence and to be "chosen" for who she is, not just what she offers.
