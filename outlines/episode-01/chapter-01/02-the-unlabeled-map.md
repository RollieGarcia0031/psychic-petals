# Scene: The Unlabeled Map

## Context
* **Location:** 11th Grade Math Classroom.
* **Characters:** Damien, Dahlia.

## Objective
Introduce Dahlia and her observant nature, contrasting with Damien's desire for privacy.

## Key Beats
* **The Detail:** Dahlia leans over to see Damien's map. It’s a 3D model with startling precision, but lacks any labels.
* **The Interaction:** Dahlia tries to decipher it, asking where "home" is. Damien gives a vague, non-committal answer.
* **The Contrast:** Dahlia’s own map is colorful and filled with people/dreams; Damien’s is an island of technical data.

## Outcome
Dahlia is intrigued by the "unlabeled" boy, while Damien feels the first prickle of unwanted attention.
