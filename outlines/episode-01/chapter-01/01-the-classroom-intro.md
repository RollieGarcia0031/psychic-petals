# Scene: The Classroom Intro

## Context
* **Location:** 11th Grade Math Classroom.
* **Characters:** Damien, Math Teacher.

## Objective
Establish the academic environment and Damien's quiet, technically proficient presence.

## Key Beats
* **The Atmosphere:** The start of the 11th-grade year. The math teacher's attempts at humor to break the first-day tension.
* **The Exercise:** The teacher assigns a "Mental Mapping" exercise—draw where you come from and where you want to go.
* **The Reaction:** Damien's immediate focus on the technical precision of his drawing rather than the emotional or social aspect.

## Outcome
The class is established as a place of standard social friction, which Damien navigated with practiced neutrality.
