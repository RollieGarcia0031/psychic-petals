# Scene: The Classroom Intro

## Context
* **Location:** 11th Grade Math Classroom.
* **Characters:** Damien, Dahlia, Math Teacher.

## Objective
Introduce the protagonist's internal world through his external actions (the map) and his reaction to social pressur
(the introduction).

## Key Beats
* **The Map:** Damien draws a 3D model instead of a simple map. It’s unlabeled—he knows where he belongs, but he
doesn't want others to.
* **The Interaction:** Dahlia notices the detail. Damien is ambiguous about his home.
* **The Intro:** Damien's hesitation about his "dream." The weight of the unspoken.
* **The Atmosphere:** The teacher's joke breaks the tension for others, but not for Damien.
* **The Blind Spot:** Dahlia tries to sit with him during recess but leaves because of the glare/poor angle. Damien
chose this seat specifically because it is a "dead zone."

## Outcome
Damien is left alone, which is exactly what he planned, even if it feels "unsettling."
