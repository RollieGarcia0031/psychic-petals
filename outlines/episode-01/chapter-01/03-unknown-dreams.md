# Scene: Unknown Dreams

## Context
* **Location:** Front of the Classroom.
* **Characters:** Damien, Dahlia, Classmates, Math Teacher.

## Objective
Show Damien's discomfort with public self-expression and his lack of a "marketable" dream.

## Key Beats
* **The Introduction:** Each student must stand and share their name and their "dream."
* **The Hesitation:** When it's Damien's turn, he falters. He doesn't want to share his actual interests (songwriting/constructs).
* **The Admission:** He admits he hasn't "figured it out" yet. It feels like a failure in a world that demands direction.
* **The Teacher's Save:** A math joke from the teacher breaks the silence, but the weight of the unspoken remains for Damien.

## Outcome
Damien is labeled as the "undecided" student, adding to his sense of being out of sync with his peers.
