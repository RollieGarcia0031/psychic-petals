# Scene: The Blind Spot

## Context
* **Location:** School Courtyard / Recess Seating.
* **Characters:** Damien, Dahlia.

## Objective
Demonstrate Damien's active isolation and Dahlia's failed attempt to bridge the gap.

## Key Beats
* **The Choice:** Damien explains (internally) why he chose his specific seat. It’s a "dead zone" where the sun’s glare makes it uncomfortable for anyone else to stay long.
* **The Attempt:** Dahlia tries to sit with him. She wants to talk about the mapping exercise.
* **The Departure:** The glare and the poor angle eventually force her to leave, as Damien planned.
* **The Aftermath:** Damien is left alone, but he notices a lingering feeling of "unsettling" peace.

## Outcome
Damien’s "defensive architecture" works, but for the first time, he wonders if he’s blocking out more than just noise.
