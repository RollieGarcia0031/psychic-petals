# Scene: Meeting Ben

## Context
* **Location:** Classroom
* **Characters:** Damien, Ben

## Objective
Introduce the character of Ben as Damien's first friend

## Key Beats
* **Arrival To School:** Damien came to the classroom and found another gut sitting beside his desk
* **Ben's First Talk:**
    * Ben introduced himself. Damien politely introduced himself.
    * Conversation:
        ```
        Ben: Hi, my name is Ben, remember me from High School?
        Damien: Yeah, kinda.
        Ben: You seem to be quiete, can I sit here?
        Damien: Yeah, sure.
        *awkward silence*
        Ben: Do you live nearby, you're always too early at class.
        Damien: Yeah, it only takes 20 minutes to walk, 5 minutes if I fly.
        Ben: So you can fly?! That's cool. I live far from here, and mostly I ride car 
              with my dad, he drops me off then go to school.
        Damien: I was just joking, I can't fly.
        Ben: Yeah, I was also joking...
        ```
* **Conversation Cutoff:** The profesor of biology will came and their conversation ended
* **His first seatmate:**
    * Ben stayed as his seatmate, and talk to him during class.
    * Conversation:
    ```
    Ben: You know, its weird that I'm interested in biology, and being nurse, but damn I'm
    scared of blood.
    Damien: *doesn't reply and focused on class*
    ```
* **In-out:** Ben felt like Damien is too serious and decided to move to other seat on the next class.

## Outcome
Ben and Damien talks a little, but Ben felt like he can't be that close to Damien as Damien seems to be more
serious in life.

# Scene: Free Ride To School

## Context
* **Location:** Side of Highway
* **Time:** Morning Before going to School
* **School Day:** Third Day of School

## Objectives
* Show Damien being awkward towards people.

## Key Beats
* **Taking Off:**
    * Damien was walking at the sideway.
    * He looks at his soroundings and found no one that recognizes him.
    * He saw other student going to school by flying/floating/different ways of levitating.
    * Some students are brought by their parents that also have the abilitity to fly.
    * Damien decided to also fly to go to school faster.
* **Cancel Take-off:**
    * A student riding motorcycle calls out Damien's name.
    * The student takes-off his helment, and Damien found it was Ben.
* **Free Ride:**
    * Ben told Damien to hop on.
    * Damien was nervous and accidentally wrapped his arms around Ben's stomach.
    * Conversation:
        ```
        Ben: Dude, is this your first time?
        Damien: Yeah, kinda.
        Ben: Just hold on dude, I won't go fast.
        Damien: *held at the back of the motorcyle*
        Ben: So, are you about to fly or something? I saw your hand's glowing...
        Damien: *can't hear Ben's voice as he is wearing a helmet*
        Damien: What?
        Ben: I said are you about to...
        Damien: What?
        ```
    * Ben decided to not talk anymore even if they already stopped at the school.
## Outcome
* Damien learned for the first time how to ride with someone's bike and try trusting other people.
