# Chapter 2: Echoes from the Stars

## Chapter Summary
Dahlia briefly reenters Damien’s life for a project. The encounter is hollow. Later that night, Damien hears a voice he hasn't heard in years.

## Narrative Objectives
* Provide a final look at the "Dahlia connection" to show how far Damien has come.
* Introduce the telepathic hook from Kai.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Group Project** - Damien and Dahlia are assigned to the same seminar. They speak politely, but the "resonance" is completely gone.
2. **Scene 02: The Clean Break** - After the meeting, Dahlia talks about her fiancé/partner. Damien feels nothing but a quiet happiness for her.
3. **Scene 03: The First Whisper** - As Damien falls asleep, he hears Kai’s voice. Not a memory, but a call.
