# Chapter 3: The Shared Nightmare

## Chapter Summary
Kai’s psychic abilities have grown, but they are leaking into Damien’s mind as nightmares. He starts seeing fragmented images of a white room and silence.

## Narrative Objectives
* Show the "Invasion of Privacy" aspect of their new connection.
* Establish the "Depression" theme through symbolic imagery.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The White Room** - Damien wakes up in a cold sweat. He has "seen" a room he’s never been to.
2. **Scene 02: The Telepathic Call** - Kai reaches out formally. She sounds "fine," but her thoughts (the ones she doesn't say) are dark.
3. **Scene 03: The Mirror** - Damien realizes that his own emotions are starting to mirror Kai’s.
