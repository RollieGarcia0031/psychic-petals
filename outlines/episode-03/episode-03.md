# Episode 3: The Cost of Being Understood

## Overview
Two years after Kai’s departure, Damien’s powers have evolved to the point of permanent matter manipulation. When Kai reaches out telepathically from Vara, Damien is pulled into her world of depression and isolation. He must learn that true understanding isn't about saving someone—it’s about knowing when to let them stand on their own.

## Themes
* **The Weight of Permanence:** The shift from fleeting imagination to lasting impact.
* **Telepathic Intimacy:** Connection across distances and the invasion of privacy/nightmares.
* **Saving vs. Supporting:** The difference between solving someone's problems and being present for them.
* **Attachment as a Chain:** Recognizing when a connection is based on need rather than growth.

## Major Milestones
1. **The Evolution (Ch 1-2):** Damien’s new powers and his return to isolation in college.
2. **The Telepathic Echo (Ch 3-4):** Kai reaches out; the start of the shared nightmares and long-distance connection.
3. **The Hospitalization (Ch 5-6):** Damien discovers Kai’s struggle with her mental health through fragmented dreams.
4. **The Construct of Comfort (Ch 7):** Damien sends permanent objects to Vara; Kai’s rejection of "solutions."
5. **The Trap of Understanding (Ch 8-9):** Realizing their attachment is becoming a crutch; the decision to distance.
6. **The Final Release (Ch 10):** Damien teaches Kai control; the quiet end and the final lesson of letting go.

## Character Goals
* **Damien:** To support Kai without trying to "fix" her, and to find a way to exist without needing to be needed.
* **Kai:** To find her own strength on Vara without relying on the telepathic tether to Earth.
