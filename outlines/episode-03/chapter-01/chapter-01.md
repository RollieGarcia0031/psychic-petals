# Chapter 1: The Architect of Atoms

## Chapter Summary
Two years later, Damien is in college. His power has evolved from fleeting constructs to permanent matter manipulation. He is more powerful, but more isolated than ever.

## Narrative Objectives
* Show the evolution of Damien’s power (manipulating atoms).
* Establish his "Social Threat" status—people are afraid of what he can do.
* Show his detachment from music and hobbies.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Lab** - Damien creates a perfect, permanent diamond from the carbon in the air during a physics lab. The professor is horrified rather than impressed.
2. **Scene 02: The Campus Cafe** - Damien sits alone. People whisper. He is no longer the "quiet boy," he is the "Anomaly."
3. **Scene 03: The Dusty Guitar** - Damien looks at his guitar in his dorm. He hasn't played in months.
