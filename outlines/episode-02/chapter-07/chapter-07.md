# Chapter 7: A Pocket of Breath

## Chapter Summary
Damien uses his powers to create a "pocket" of adjusted air for Kai. It’s a small, intimate act of care that doesn't fix her, but helps her endure.

## Narrative Objectives
* Demonstrate Damien’s evolving power (creating invisible, complex constructs like "adjusted air").
* Show the intimacy of this specific help—it’s something only he can do for her.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Experiment** - Damien practices manipulating the atoms in a small area. It’s exhausting.
2. **Scene 02: The Gift** - Damien walks with Kai. He creates the pocket around her. Her eyes widen as she finally breathes without effort.
3. **Scene 03: The Moment of Peace** - They sit in the "pocket," a literal bubble of safety in a world that is too thin for her.
