# Chapter 1: The Neutral Ground

## Chapter Summary
On Dahlia’s 18th birthday, Damien meets her one last time. It’s an ordinary meeting that proves they’ve both moved on.

## Narrative Objectives
* Provide closure for the Season 1 arc.
* Show Damien’s growth: he is still quiet, but no longer "drowning."
* Introduce the concept of "acknowledgment" versus "longing."

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Park Bench** - Damien and Dahlia meet. The air is clear. No tension, just two people who once knew each other.
2. **Scene 02: The 33** - Damien shows her the music he wrote. Not as a plea, but as a gift of "this was real."
3. **Scene 03: The Parting** - They walk in opposite directions. Damien feels a sense of completion he didn't have before.
