# Chapter 3: The Single Frequency

## Chapter Summary
Kai realizes that Damien is the only person she can read. The anomaly draws them together in a way neither expected.

## Narrative Objectives
* Explain the "Anomaly": why only Damien?
* Show the discomfort and fascination Kai feels with this sudden clarity.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Crowd** - Kai is overwhelmed by the "silence" of other people’s minds. Then she hears Damien’s thoughts like a clear bell.
2. **Scene 02: The Confrontation** - Kai tracks Damien down. She doesn't tell him she's reading him, but her questions are too specific.
3. **Scene 03: The Shared Silence** - They sit together in the library. Damien is thinking about a melody; Kai "hears" it and starts humming along. Damien is stunned.
