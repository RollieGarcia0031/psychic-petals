# Chapter 4: Unfiltered Clarity

## Chapter Summary
Around Kai, Damien feels he doesn't have to perform. Their bond grows through her ability to "see" him without distortion.

## Narrative Objectives
* Show Damien’s relief at being understood without explanation.
* Contrast this with his past with Dahlia, where he was always "testing."

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Truth Revealed** - Kai admits she can hear his thoughts (only his). Damien’s reaction is surprisingly calm.
2. **Scene 02: No Performance Required** - They spend a day together. Damien doesn't hide his "weird" habits; Kai encourages him to be himself.
3. **Scene 03: The First Song of Season 2** - Damien writes a song that is no longer abstract, but clear and sincere.
