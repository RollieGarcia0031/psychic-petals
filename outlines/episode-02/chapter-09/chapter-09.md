# Chapter 9: The Incomplete Truth

## Chapter Summary
As the school year ends, Damien finally speaks his feelings. It’s honest, but he leaves out the parts where he thinks they could actually work.

## Narrative Objectives
* Capture the "Incomplete Confession" beat.
* Show the growth in Damien—he *does* speak this time, even if it’s not "brave" enough.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Final Walk** - They walk through the campus one last time. The air is heavy (metaphorically).
2. **Scene 02: The Confession** - Damien tells Kai he cares about her. He doesn't ask her to stay. He doesn't offer a solution.
3. **Scene 03: The Truth of Vara** - Kai tells him she is bound to someone else on Vara—a historical and social expectation she cannot break.
