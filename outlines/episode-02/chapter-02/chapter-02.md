# Chapter 2: The Girl Who Doesn't Disappear

## Chapter Summary
Kai, a transfer student from Vara, arrives at Damien’s college. She is sarcastic, witty, and perpetually exhausted.

## Narrative Objectives
* Introduce Kai and her "bad stamina" facade.
* Contrast Kai’s outgoing nature with her hidden physical strain.
* Establish the rumors surrounding Damien in this new environment.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Lecture Hall** - Kai takes a seat near Damien. She makes a sarcastic comment about the professor.
2. **Scene 02: The Staircase** - Damien sees Kai leaning against a wall after a flight of stairs. She jokes about being "lazy."
3. **Scene 03: The First Spark** - A moment where Kai looks at Damien, and for a split second, the "static" in her head clears.
