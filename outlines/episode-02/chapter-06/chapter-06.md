# Chapter 6: The Thinning Air

## Chapter Summary
Kai’s health seems to decline. Damien notices the patterns she tries to hide—the shallow breathing, the chest clutching.

## Narrative Objectives
* Highlight the physical toll Earth takes on Varans.
* Show Damien’s observational skills returning, but now with a desire to help rather than test.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Run** - They try to catch a bus. Kai collapses, not dangerously, but utterly drained.
2. **Scene 02: The Observation** - Damien watches Kai when she thinks no one is looking. He sees the effort she puts into every breath.
3. **Scene 03: The Question** - Damien asks her directly. Kai tries to deflect, but his "Varan-aware" mind sees through it.
