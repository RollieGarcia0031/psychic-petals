# Chapter 5: The Varan Legacy

## Chapter Summary
Damien’s grandfather’s funeral leads to a discovery: his great-great-grandmother was Varan. He realizes his own connection to the stars.

## Narrative Objectives
* Provide the "Varan Legacy" backstory.
* Show Damien’s internal shift: he’s not just a "weird human," he’s part of something else.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Attic** - Damien finds old letters and a strange, glowing artifact from his grandfather’s belongings.
2. **Scene 02: The Realization** - He connects his manifestation power to Varan psionics.
3. **Scene 03: Sharing with Kai** - Damien tells Kai about his discovery. She is the first person who truly understands the weight of it.
