# Chapter 10: The Resonance of Separation

## Chapter Summary
Kai returns to Vara. They part with an understanding that some loves are meant to be rare and short-lived.

## Narrative Objectives
* Show Damien’s ability to endure loss without collapse.
* Establish the "almost-love" as a valuable thing in itself.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Departure** - The spaceport (or just the goodbye at the station). No drama, just quiet acknowledgment.
2. **Scene 02: The Empty Pocket** - Damien stands where they used to sit. He manifests the adjusted air one last time, even though no one is there to breathe it.
3. **Scene 03: The New Song** - Damien starts writing again. This time, it’s not for the world or even for Kai—it’s for himself.
