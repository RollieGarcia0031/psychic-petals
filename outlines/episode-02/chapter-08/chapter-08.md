# Chapter 8: The Weight of Expectations

## Chapter Summary
Damien learns about the harsh reality of life on Vara—the psychic rankings, the physical expectations. He begins to fear that he can never give her what she truly needs.

## Narrative Objectives
* Introduce the "merciless" nature of Varan society.
* Show Damien’s return to his old habits of withdrawal, but now out of fear for *her* future, not his own ego.

## Scene List
1. **Scene 01: The Video Call** - Kai gets a call from her parents on Vara. Damien overhears the cold, demanding tone.
2. **Scene 02: The Comparison** - Damien realizes that on Vara, he would be a "halfling" and Kai is "weak." The weight of their differences feels insurmountable.
3. **Scene 03: The Retreat** - Damien starts skipping their meetings. He tells himself it’s to "let her focus on her future."
